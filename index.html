About Caliche

Caliche isn't just a DJ—he's a curator of moments. With years of experience spinning tracks across venues and events, his signature style blends the nostalgia of freestyle and 80s hits with the contemporary edge of hip-hop, R&B, and pop.

Whether you're hosting an exclusive club night, a corporate event, or a private celebration, Caliche reads the room and crafts an unforgettable sonic journey. His technical skills on the decks are matched only by his ability to connect emotionally with every beat.

From underground 90s hip-hop to chart-topping pop anthems, Caliche brings passion, precision, and personality to every set. Let's turn your event into an experience.
